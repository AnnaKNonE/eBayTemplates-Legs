Timeless stability

These hairpin legs unite strong sturdiness with timeless elegance and give every piece of furniture an extra touch of exclusivity. Handmade from 10 mm steel, these hairpin legs by Hardman Design & Build classily segue into an angled shape. The solid, hand-formed pattern bestows our in-house hairpin leg collection with resilience and longevity. Our exclusive two- and three-leg-designs offer legs that add an extra flavour of retro to any room.

Natural quality

All Hardman Design & Build products are delivered with rust proof finish

We can produce your original Hardman Design & Build hairpin legs from dark raw steel (also referred to as black steel) or from cold rolled silver steel, which is 10 mm thick and is hand-formed by us, according to your taste. All Hardman Design & Build products are delivered with rust proof finish. The material surface is refined with high quality CLOU bees wax. The organic components of this natural finish are especially suited to protect steel in interior areas and is also used as a finish for children’s toys. The professional finish with wax protects the steel from moisture and also acts as an antistatic, which keeps dirt away from your hairpin legs – without chemical solutions! Here at Hardman Design & Build we put great emphasis on high quality, environmental-friendly and natural finishing for our products. And we offer you support in caring for your hairpin legs!

Individuality

Our 40 cm-version is perfectly suited for smaller benches, side tables and coffee tables, while the 72 cm-version is made for desks or dining tables. An ideal way to customise furniture, since the legs by Hardman Design & Build are available in a two- and three-rod design. For an additional charge, our hairpin legs can also be spray painted in different colours according to customer requests. Give free reign to your phantasies! With an extra coating of hard wax oil varnish, these gracious hairpin legs can also be used to enhance your garden or balcony furniture. Let us know about your wishes with your order or write us if you are looking for individually customised furniture.

 

Frame colors:

Raw steel = Purist raw steel with phosphate surface. The natural colour of the material is maintained in this process and varies from dark gray to black.

Silver Steel = polished bright steel with silver surface. The surface treatment used here consists of descaling and chip-less cold forming. The result is characterized by a flat and smooth surface.

Coloured finish = Our legs can be spray painted in your favourite colour for an additional charge. Simply let us know over an email which Ral-colour-number you prefer! For an overview of our colour selection please click here: RAL Colour Palette

 
 

About us

At Hardman Design & Build, attention to detail is our main focus. We are a small group of young craftspeople who have joined forces in 2010 in Berlin. Furniture made from finest wood and elegant table legs make their way into the wide world from our colourful workshop near the river Spree in Berlin. The genuine Hardman Design & Build hairpin legs are the fastest runners: the standard issue can be with you as fast as three to five days after receipt of payment! Every piece is made by hand and the entire production process takes place in-house. Should you have special requests or should you be dissatisfied with one of our products, we would like to ask you to contact us. Together we will find the best possible solution!
